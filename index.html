THE TEMPLEMAN REVIEW



The Templeman Review is an online literary magazine run by students at the University of Kent. It is published termly and is free to download from this website.




Click here to read Issue Number One, Autumn Term 2020.



Submissions 


We accept submissions from everyone, including non-students and people unaffiliated with the University of Kent. All submissions from UKC students should include a college affiliation. We offer no guidelines concerning length or style. All submissions should be original, and we cannot accept submissions that have been previously published, except for on personal blogs. We allow simultaneous submissions provided you promptly withdraw your work if it is accepted elsewhere. Please send work as an attachment in .pdf or .docx format to:

submissions@thetemplemanreview.com
