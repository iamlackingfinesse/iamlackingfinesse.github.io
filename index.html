The Templeman Review

 

The Templeman Review is an online literary magazine run by students at the University of Kent. It is published termly and is free to download from this website.

Submissions For the Autumn Issue

The Templeman Review seeks original fiction, non-fiction, poetry, and literary reviews. In particular, The Templeman Review aims to amplify the voices of undergraduates who would otherwise not have had the opportunity to have been published. Submissions from postgraduates and non-students will also be considered. We offer no guidelines concerning length or style. All submissions should be original, and we cannot accept submissions that have been previously published. We allow simultaneous submissions provided you promptly withdraw your work if it is accepted elsewhere.

Submissions should be sent as an attachment in .pdf or .docx format to thetemplemanreview@gmail.com.

They should also include a name, and if a student at the University of Kent, a college affiliation. This can be found under 'my record' in the student data system.

The deadline for submissions for the autumn issue is 17/10/2020.
