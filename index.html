THE TEMPLEMAN REVIEW



The Templeman Review is an online literary magazine run by students at the University of Kent. It is published termly and is free to download from this website.

Submissions for future issues

 The Templeman Review accepts submissions from everyone, including non-students and people unaffiliated with the University of Kent. However, please be aware that the work of Kent undergraduates will usually take priority. All submissions from UKC students should include a college affiliation. We offer no guidelines concerning length or style, and don't worry too much about formatting or references. All submissions should be original, and we cannot accept submissions that have been previously published, except for on personal blogs. We allow simultaneous submissions provided you promptly withdraw your work if it is accepted elsewhere. Please send work as an attachment in .pdf or .docx format to:

submissions@thetemplemanreview.com
